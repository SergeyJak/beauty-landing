### 4. **Hair Growth Phases Section** 🆕 NEW
Educational section explaining why multiple treatments needed

**Three Phases Explained**:

**Anagen (Growth Phase)** - 85% of hair
- Hair actively grows from follicle
- ONLY phase when permanent treatment possible
- Lasts 2-7 years
- Most treatable

**Catagen (Transition Phase)** - 2-3% of hair
- Hair transitions from growth to rest
- Follicle begins to separate
- Lasts 2-3 weeks
- Not treatable

**Telogen (Resting Phase)** - 10-15% of hair
- Hair rests in follicle before shedding
- Follicle dormant
- Cannot be treated during this phase
- Lasts 5-14 weeks

**Treatment Timeline Visualization**:
- 1st Treatment → Treats 85% in Anagen
- 2-3 Follow-ups → Treats newly visible hairs
- ∞ Permanent Result → No more hair growth
